# SEO Roadmap: Beauty Salon

This document maps the search strategy to the site structure to ensure maximum organic visibility.

## 3. Local SEO Anchors
- **Primary Location**: Antelope Valley (Palmdale/Lancaster)
- **Supporting Areas**: Quartz Hill, Rosamond, Lake Los Angeles, Littlerock, Santa Clarita

## 4. Content Matrix
- **Homepage**: Focus on full-service luxury, transformation, and relaxation.
- **Service Pages**: Detail specific beauty categories (Hair, Skin, Makeup, Lashes).
- **Location Pages**: Localized beauty guides and wedding venue partnerships.
