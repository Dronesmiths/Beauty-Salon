Eye Enhancements

Your eyes are the focal point of your face. We help you frame them perfectly with expert brow shaping and lash enhancements that save you time on your morning routine.

Our Beauty Bar Services

  • Brow Shaping & Waxing: Sculpting the perfect arch to flatter your face.
  • Brow Lamination: A perm for your brows to give them a set, uniform shape (fluffy brow look).
